The Haunted Mansion
Easy
Five ghost hunters have just spent time in an allegedly haunted mansion in Whispering Pines, investigating paranormal activity reported by the new owner. Once inside the expansive home, which boasts 20 rooms, the ghost hunters split up and each had a chilling experience! From the clues provided, can you determine the experience each person had and the room they were in at the time?
The Haunted Mansion - Clues
1. Shadow Chaser didn't see any spooky shadows during his time in the house. The shadows were not seen in the attic.
2. Neither the ghost hunter who saw a book fall from a shelf nor Mr Apparition had their ghostly experiences in the attic.
3. It was either Spirit Snoopervisor or Hairy Scary who had a memorable experience in the wine cellar - which was filled floor to ceiling with antiques and collectors items, but no wine!
4. While taking some time in the main bedroom to text his wife about dinner, one startled investigator heard slow moving footsteps on the squeaky stairs that he confirmed could not have come from any of the others in the house.
5. Hairy Scary earned her name due to her long, jet-black hair. While she may have looks akin to those of an actress from a Japanese horror film, she was entirely unprepared for the shock of hearing eerie voices on the radio device she was using!
6. One man shuddered when later telling the tale of how he felt a hand touch his shoulder, only to turn and find no one there! This did not occur in the wine cellar nor the living room.
7. Compared to one of his colleagues, Mr Apparition had a very ordinary time when he passed through the hallway.
8. Specter Sleuth maintains that the room was very still and he was nowhere near the bookcase when the book suddenly fell to the floor. It was a book about ornithology, which he found a little ironic given that it virtually flew by itself.
9. Of the event that occurred in the hallway and the sighting of strange shadows, one was experienced by Spirit Snoopervisor and the other was experienced by Shadow Chaser.
Pity the Passwords
Easy
Pieter's first task of his new job at PR firm "Scandaless" is to organize his supervisor's most commonly used passwords. First he needs to reset them all in case his predecessor is a little bitter (also simply because she came up with terrible passwords), then he needs to record them in a secure location (like a logic puzzle!). From the clues provided, can you determine the old password and the new password for each type of application?
Pity the Passwords - Clues
1. Pieter was a little amused at the use of passwords "password2" and "password3", which were the passwords used for the Client Management and the Social Media applications, but not necessarily in that order.
2. The password "kittycats" was promptly changed to a more secure password, starting with a capital letter V.
3. The two former passwords that were simply the word "password" followed by a number have now been changed to a more secure choice! Neither of them were changed to a password beginning with the lowercase letter "t".
4. As Pieter snickered at his predecessor's use of the password "thisisdumb" for the Document Sharing application, he wondered what he was getting himself into with this new job. Hopefully she was just bored that day.
5. The new password for the Video Production application has at least one non-alphanumeric symbol in it. This is also the case for the Social Media application and the Backup Manager.
6. Three of the unique applications are the one that had its password changed to "bK9FByy7KtFjAm", the one that formerly had the password "password3", and the Client Management application.
7. The new password for the Document Sharing application does not have an @ symbol in it. The new password for the Backup Manager does not end with the dollar symbol ($).
8. Pieter employed a creative expression of his name for the Note Taking application password, but after his new supervisor was not amused he used a random password generator for the others.
9. The Note Taking application was either the one with old password "123abc" or the one with new password "tEp@x2&N8fdayS".
10. Of the Social Media application and the Video Production application, one has a new password beginning with lowercase letter "k", and the other previously had the password "bingo".
Highway Murder Mystery
Medium
A killer is on the run! Police are in hot pursuit, and the fugitive has taken to the woods along a rural highway. Local residents have found evidence of his path, but officers scribbled down bits of information in a rush. Match each piece of evidence to the mile marker where it was found and the witness who found it to help track the killer.
Highway Murder Mystery - Clues
1. Either the forest ranger or the bloodied shirt were near mile marker 86.
2. The lady walking her dog (a schnauzer cross poodle, otherwise known as a schnoodle) didn't spot any shoe prints.
3. The victim's hair, the item found near mile marker 85, and the clues discovered by the hiker and the scout troop are four different pieces of evidence.
4. The mile marker where the scout troop made camp was one mile more than where the hiker found her piece of evidence.
5. The suspect abandoned his blood stained shirt near mile marker 82.
6. A cigarette butt in the suspect's favorite brand was found at the mile marker one mile less than where the strands of hair were found.
7. The murder weapon was found at mile marker 86. It was purchased earlier that day and still had a price sticker on the handle.
Stings & Scrapes Tattoo Studio
Medium
Stings And Scrapes Tattoo Studio is fully booked this Friday for seven members of the local motorcycle club, The Rollicking Riders. The club is comprised of all kinds of motorcycle enthusiasts just having a bit of fun and enjoying the company! Can you identify the appointment time of each member (identified by their club nickname) and the tattoo design they have chosen?
Stings & Scrapes Tattoo Studio - Clues
1. Riffraff's appointment is earlier in the day than Gunner's. He's quietly afraid of the pain, and wants to get it over with as soon as possible.
2. The person with the 2:00pm appointment time, Medusa, the person getting the flames design, the person with the 9:00am appointment, and the person getting the serpent design are five different people.
3. Poison is booked in for a colorful roses design, but not at 3:00pm as he will be busy picking his kids up from school.
4. Knuckles has his appointment immediately before Poison, and immediately after Riffraff.
5. Of Knuckles and the one who is getting the motorcycle tattoo, one has an appointment at 11:00am and the other has an appointment at 3:00pm.
6. Little Hawk has either the first appointment of the day or the last appointment of the day.
7. The handlebars design will be sometime before the skulls design, which will be sometime before the serpent design.
8. Little Hawk is not getting the handlebars design nor the tribal pattern.
9. The flames design will be tattooed earlier in the day than the handlebars.
10. Medusa, who hates skull tattoos and would never get one, does not have a 10:00am appointment.
Spilling the Tea
Hard
Myenda Insurance Group has operated with the same management team for two decades, many of whom are not popular among the company's employees. The staff turnover rate is high, and new employees are not always well vetted! Between controversial decisions and inconsistent treatment of employees, rumors are always circulating in the head office and employees make it through the day by exchanging the latest gossip. This week, five employees in various positions have been discussed at length, each for a unique reason.
Spilling the Tea - Clues
1. The five rumors are the one about an unfair pay raise, the one about the Auditor, the one about Cassandra, the one started by Quinn, and the one about the Account Manager.
2. Noah does not get along well with Tomas, but due to their desks being close he overheard some juicy information when Tomas was on the phone to his girlfriend this week. Noah just had to spill the tea to his favorite colleagues!
3. Lydia wasn't the one to start the rumor about the HR Manager stealing lunches, but she wasn't surprised when she heard it. Yesterday, her delicious leftover beef ragout mysteriously disappeared from the staff fridge. Its scent still lingered from the microwave... right before the manager's lunch meeting.
4. It somehow made it through the grapevine that a certain staff member had received a generous pay raise, right at a time where other staff had been told of a pay freeze! The rumored recipient is neither Estelle nor Isabel.
5. Neither Lydia nor Quinn were responsible for the gossip about Isabel.
6. Helena is either the one who started the rumor about Rahul or the one who started the rumor about the Claims Adjuster.
7. Usually management don't get involved in the gossip that makes its way around the office, but when they heard that Ryan had been suggesting to others that a certain staff member was having an affair with their boss, a company-wide email went out and Ryan was given a warning.
8. Tomas is neither a Claims Adjuster nor a Claims Examiner.
9. One talking point for the week was that one of the staff had been observed sneaking out from work early every day for the past month. This was neither Estelle nor the Account Manager.
10. Lydia did not talk about the Claims Adjuster.
11. Of the person rumored to have lied on their resume and the Auditor, one is Isabel and the other was first talked about by Noah.
Sleepydale Radio Fresh Talent
Hard
The local community radio station in the small city of Sleepydale has just run its annual Fresh Talent competition, accepting song submissions from new bands and musical artists. The six finalists are each in a different genre of music. Can you determine the name of each artist, their genre, the name of their track that made the finals, and the length of each track (in minutes:seconds format)?
Sleepydale Radio Fresh Talent - Clues
1. The six songs are the one by Hitch Atom, the one that goes for exactly 2 minutes 34 seconds, "City Of Dreams", "Strained Slumber", the one by Met A Man, and the EDM track.
2. Of "Darkest Days" and the song by Met A Man, one is 3:36 long and the other is a Pop song.
3. "One Eye Open" plays for longer than Tommy T. Tom's track.
4. The Country song has a shorter duration than Sleepy Wall's offering.
5. It's no accident that "One Eye Open" is precisely 4 minutes long, with the artist being a fan of round numbers.
6. No song starts with the first letter of its artist's name.
7. Of "City Of Dreams" and the Hitch Atom song, one is a Folk song and the other is 2:58 long.
8. There is a difference in duration of 1 minute 2 seconds between the Hip Hop song and the song by The Fireneers.
9. The Rock song is 14 seconds longer than the Country song.
10. Faster Slower's song is 24 seconds longer than "Halfway Here".
11. "Darkest Days" is not a Hip Hop nor a Folk song.
12. Faster Slower's song is longer than the Pop song, but shorter than Sleepy Wall's song.
13. "After Midnight", which is not a Hip Hop song, was not recorded by The Fireneers, Tommy T. Tom, nor Met A Man.
