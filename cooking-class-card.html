Cooking Class
Last night three friends who have just taken a cooking class held a dinner to practice their new skills. Can you identify which person brought which main course, and the dessert they each made for the evening?

Clues:

1. The three people are Diego, the one who brought the Carne Asada, and the one who brought the Tres Leches Cake.
2. Sofia did not bring the Mole Chicken.
3. The Fish Tacos were brought by either Diego or the person who brought the Flan.

Cooking Class - Solving Strategy

Step-by-Step Approach:

Start with Clue 1: "The three people are Diego, the one who brought the Carne Asada, and the one who brought the Tres Leches Cake."

This means Diego is different from both the Carne Asada person and the Tres Leches Cake person. So Diego brought neither Carne Asada nor made Tres Leches Cake.

Use Clue 2: "Sofia did not bring the Mole Chicken."

So Sofia brought either Carne Asada or Fish Tacos.

Apply Clue 3: "The Fish Tacos were brought by either Diego or the person who brought the Flan."

Combined with what we know, this helps narrow down who brought what.
