7. The Detective Challenge
Answer: Alex ate the pizza
Deductive reasoning:
  • Only ONE person tells the truth
  • If Drew tells truth: Casey lies ‚Üí Drew innocent
  • If Blake tells truth: He was in class ‚Üí Blake innocent
  • If Alex tells truth: Blake guilty ‚Üí But Blake was in class (contradiction)
  • Therefore: Drew tells truth, Alex lied to frame Blake
8. The Secret Word Cipher
Answer: PIANO
Decoding process:
  • Each letter shifted forward one: A‚ÜíB, B‚ÜíC, etc.
  • To decode, shift backward one position
  • Q ‚Üí P, J ‚Üí I, B ‚Üí A, O ‚Üí N, P ‚Üí O
  • Result: PIANO
9. The Schedule Solver
Answer: They cannot meet!

  • Before 2pm: Carlos working
  • 2-3pm: Only 1 hour (need 2)
  • 3-5pm: Ana has soccer practice
  • 4-5:30pm: Ben at dentist

No 2-hour window works for all four friends!

üü° Medium Problems
